# Resetting your password
If you forget your password, you can reset it from the [sign-in page](https://complib.org/signin). 

Enter the email address which is linked to your account into the Email address field, and then click **Reset password**. Instructions for how to set up a new password for your account will be sent to this address.

## Forgotten email address, etc
If you have forgotten which email was originally linked to your account, or are otherwise unable to access your account for any reason, then you are advised to get in touch directly by sending an email to [support@complib.org](mailto:support@complib.org).
